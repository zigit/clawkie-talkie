HOW IT WORKS

Three taps. Zero friction.

One persistent local daemon does the heavy lifting — transcription, reasoning, and speech synthesis — so the phone is just a microphone and a speaker.

01 · INSTALL

Tell your agent to install.

Copy the install instructions and paste them into your agent. Your agent follows AGENT-INSTALL.md, inspects the repo first, then installs the persistent daemon and OpenClaw clawkie-voice-handoff skill.

02 · HANDOFF

Say "switch to voice."

From any OpenClaw turn, the agent hands you a deterministic rendezvous URL built from the current session. Open it on the phone in your pocket — that's the only thing you do.

03 · TALK

Speak. Hear the reply.

Tap once to record, again to send. Live transcription streams as you speak; the reply is read back through your earbuds.

FEATURES

Built like instrumentation, not a chatbot.

Every detail — the OLED palette, the mono caption labels, the breathing aura on the record button — is tuned so you can read state at a glance, mid-conversation.

OPENCLAW INFER

Words appear as you say them.

OpenClaw infer produces full-turn transcripts, while WASM VAD committed chunks keep the live caption moving before the turn is finalized.

NATURAL TTS

Reply, read aloud.

Daemon-side text-to-speech streams audio back to the phone with low latency. Adjustable rate. Skip mid-sentence with a tap.

POCKET-FRIENDLY

Tap-to-talk, tuned for motion.

A huge press-to-talk control, live captions, and replay make it easy to use while walking, cooking, or working away from the keyboard.

LOCAL DAEMON

One persistent session.

Your local daemon owns the WebRTC, STT, and TTS sessions. Pull out a different phone mid-conversation and the session follows you, not the device.

USE CASES

For when typing isn't an option.

DRIVING

Pair-program from the car.

Talk through a deploy, walk through a stack trace, or dictate the next commit message — without taking your eyes off the road.

WALKING

Think out loud on a walk.

Long-form reasoning works better when you can move. Speak the question, listen to the reply, refine, repeat.

FOCUSING

Voice handoff from chat.

You're deep in a thread. Say "switch to voice." Keep going without breaking the rhythm of the conversation.
